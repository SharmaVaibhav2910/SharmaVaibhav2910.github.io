Welcome to VK LMS , the only site where you can a truly good education.

    Personalized Learning Journeys
    
Every student has a Knowledge Graph based on their learning need & speed. 
This links different concepts and helps students identify their strengths & 
areas for improvement.

    Mapped to the Syllabus
    
Learning journeys are mapped to a student's grade & syllabus (CBSE). Each 
chapter comprises small concepts that are stitched together with adaptive 
questions as per the relevant syllabus.

    Unlimited Practice & In-depth Analysis
    
Adaptive, interactive tests that help students master each chapter. 
Recommended videos prompt students to correct mistakes in a practice session, 
enabling long lasting learning.
